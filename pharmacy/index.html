Key Features

Inventory Tracking

Keep tabs on your pharmacy inventory in real-time. Easily monitor stock levels, track expiration dates, and receive alerts for low stock items, ensuring you never run out of essential medications.

Order Management

Simplify the ordering process with PharmaTrack's automated system. Generate purchase orders, track deliveries, and manage suppliers seamlessly, saving time and reducing errors.

Custom Reports

Tailor reports to suit your pharmacy's needs. Generate comprehensive reports on sales, inventory turnover, and more. Analyze data to make informed decisions and optimize your operations for greater efficiency.
