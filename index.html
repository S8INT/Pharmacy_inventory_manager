About

The Pharmacy Inventory Manager project was inspired by a real-world problem faced by many pharmacies. During my time working close with my friends, I noticed how challenging it was to keep track of inventory manually. This led to frequent stockouts and overstock situations, impacting the quality of service provided to customers. I decided to create a solution that would automate inventory management, making it more efficient and reliable for my friends to forcus on service delivery rather than worrying about the stock taking and all the frastruations that come with it.

This project was developed over the course of 1 month as a Portfolio Project for ALX Software engineering end of foundations. It showcases the skills and knowledge I have gained in software development and project management.
